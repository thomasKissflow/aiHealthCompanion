# ✅ Graceful Farewells Added!

## What I Added

Added graceful goodbye and thank you responses to the AI Health Companion.

## New Farewell Responses

When you say:
- "bye" or "goodbye"
- "thank you" or "thanks"
- "thanks for your help"
- "take care"
- "gotta go"
- "that's all"

The AI will respond with one of these warm farewells:
1. "Take care! Feel free to reach out anytime you need support."
2. "Goodbye! Remember, I'm here whenever you need to talk about your health."
3. "Thanks for chatting with me. Wishing you good health and wellness!"
4. "You're very welcome! Take care of yourself, and don't hesitate to come back if you need anything."
5. "I'm glad I could help. Stay well, and reach out anytime!"

## How It Works

- **Fast Path**: Farewell responses are instant (no LLM call)
- **Varied Responses**: Randomly selects from 5 different farewell messages
- **Natural**: Recognizes many ways to say goodbye or thank you
- **Graceful**: Warm, caring tone that matches the health companion personality

## Test It

Just restart your backend and try:

```bash
cd backend
python main.py
```

Then in the frontend:
1. Click "Connect Backend"
2. Click "🎤 Speak"
3. Say "thank you for your help"
4. Hear the warm farewell response!

Or try:
- "bye"
- "goodbye"
- "thanks"
- "take care"

All will get appropriate, graceful responses!
